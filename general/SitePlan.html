Site Plan Checklist

Project Project Number
A. House

Either import the house model from our Homebuilder Houses.dwg file, double checking garage orientation; or draft a house model from the foundation plan of the architectural plans. Rotate the house to match the lot orientation.

Verify the setbacks for the property. Place the building centered between the left and right property line and the front setback line + 1.00 foot.

Townhomes consideration: When placing a townhome on a lot, also verify that the seperation walls fall on the property line.

Provide a building tie on each side of the building (minimum one) perpendicular to that side's property line. The tie will be located on a notable point of the building (building corner or intersection). If a tie falls outside of the property, a building tie is not required.

Extend the driveway lines to meet the edge of pavement line. Keep the flairs of the driveway intact.

B. Grading

Unless otherwise specified, the FFE for the proposed house is 1.50' above the crown of the road at the middle of the lot.

With the following considerations, determine elevations for the lot and place proposed elevations where required.

  • Primary Consideration Ground flow next the road (flow left or flow right).
  • Primary Consideration Current ground elevations.
  • Primary Consideration Neighbor ground elevations.
  • Primary Consideration Swale and berm requirements.
  • Secondary Consideration Retention pond or other water body adjacent to parcel.
  • Secondary Consideration Allowed drainages (Type-A, Type-B, Type-C).

For the list of considerations, please view the online checklist.

Add drainage arrows in between proposed elevations. Rotate arrows to show intended flow direction.

C. Paperspace

Set the text directly below the logo to say "SITE PLAN".

Add drainage arrows in between proposed elevations. Rotate arrows to show intended flow direction.

Add a site plan date to Surveyor's notes.

Surveyor's Note 2
Add onto the existing note with "; SITE PLAN [DATE]"
Sample: DATE OF FIELD SURVEY: 01/01/2022; SITE PLAN 02/01/2022
