General Services

Boundary Survey

Locate property corners of both the subject parcel and neighboring parcels and rights-of-ways. We will replace any missing property corners on your parcel and flag each corner for easy locating. Locate buildings, appurtenances, features, and utilities located on and near the property. No elevations are provided.

Boundary & Topo Survey
  • Boundary See Boundary Survey; and
  • Topographic (Topo) Provides elevations on applicable features (homes, utilities, and appurtenances) along with ground elevations.

Ordering Note The client will need to confirm either the municipality or the grid size for elevations. Different municipalities require different ground shot information.

Boundary, Topo & Tree Survey
  • Boundary See Boundary Survey; and
  • Topographic (Topo) See Topographic (Topo); and
  • Tree Locates trees on the property.

Ordering Note The client will need to confirm either the municipality or the minimum size and location limits for the tree locations.

Mortgage Survey

A small modification on the boundary survey, the timing and required items are considered differently. Mortgage surveys are for the express purpose of selling a residential house and includes additional certifications.

Ordering Note The client will need to confirm either the municipality or the minimum size and location limits for the tree locations.

ALTA / NSPS

An ALTA / NSPS Land Title Survey is a survey for the selling of commercial land. The survey has more certifications, requirements, and work than a regular mortgage or boundary survey. An ALTA survey is governed by the National Society of Professional Surveyors (NSPS).

Ordering Note A Title Search, Table B-II Exception copies, and filled out table A must be provided prior to scheduling.

Elevation Certificate

An elevation certificate is a FEMA form provided for buildings located in flood plains. Elevation Certificates require a field visit by the surveyor to gather elevations on the buildings, adjacent grades, and any stairs or machinery of the property. There are three stages available for an elevation certificate:

  • Pre-Construction* Before land is cleared or developed has started.
  • Mid-Construction* Middle of construction (usually at the formboard / foundation stage).
  • Final Building is complete and final grades are set.

If a pre-construction or mid-construction EC is requested, a final EC will be required at an additional charge

Homebuilder Services

Site Plan / Plot Plan

Surveyors are allowed to provide basic site plans for residential lots. Site plans may contain a proposed building(s), driveway, septic system, basic utilities, and grading information. Surveyors are not allowed to design drainage systems or more complex sites and we will refer you to a civil engineer.

Site Plan Site plans are created after a boundary & amp; topo survey and will have references to benchmarks and grading.

Plot Plan Plot plans are created before a boundary & topo survey is performed. Will not have any boundary information, grades, or benchmarks.

Rough Stake

Rough stakes are used before the lot has been rough graded to show where the building will go and what elevation it needs to be. Lathes are used to mark building envelopes instead of hubs for costs- and time-saving measures. Includes both direct building envelope layout and offsets to the points.

Hub & Tack

Hub and tacks are used for the builder to establish their formboards and ensure the boards are at finished grade. Includes a hub at each building envelope corner and two offset lathes.

Formboard / Foundation

Formboards and foundations are used for verifying the location of a building prior to the builder going vertical.

Formboards Locate the wooden boards before the builder pours concrete.

Foundation The foundation of a building after concrete is poured and set and before anything vertical is installed.

Final

A final survey is performed after grading is completed and locates any porches, A/C pads, driveway, and above ground utilities. Includes showing the finished grades.

Other Staking

Lot Stake

Stakes the corners of the property, typically for lot clearing purposes.

Culvert Stake

Stakes the swale and culvert locations for specific grading by the builder.

Grade Stake

Places stakes at each corner and every notable grade break for the purpose of grading the lot.

Driveway Stake

Stakes offsets to the proposed driveway for the formboards to be placed for pouring.

Legal Services

These services are usually recorded with the local clerk of court of the property and goes through county reviews.

Sketch & Description

We write a legal description and provide a Sketch of Legal Description for the purpose of providing easements or other required descriptions.

Lot Split

Splits a larger lot into smaller lots and writes legal descriptions for the newly created pieces. Does not usually require a sketch for recording nor does it follow a specific law, statute, code, or ordinance.

Plat

We provide platting services compliant to §177 (Part 1), Florida Statutes and applicable county codes and ordinances. Includes filing of appropriate paperwork and coordination with other professions and services. We are not responsible to pay any fees related to applying or filing a plat.

Construction Services

Building Contractor Layout

Layout services related to a commercial building contractor or general contractor. Services may include

  • Site Calibration and setup.
  • Additional benchmarks established.
  • Layout of building envelopes up to hub & tacks.
  • Elevator pits, dumpster pads, and amenity center items.
Site Contractor Layout

Layout services related to a commercial building site contractor. Services may include

  • Site Calibration and setup.
  • Layout of utilities including water, sanitary sewer, storm, and others (including offsets of structures).
  • Layout of building envelopes up to rough staking.
  • Layout of curbing and drive isles, sidewalks, and ADA areas.
As-Builts

Certified as-built survey showing newly installed utilities, grading, drainage, and site improvements. Complies with municipality requirements and ordinances.

Machine Control

We can provide a machine control file with site calibrations for automatic machines. Files generated from Trimble Business Center.

Additional Services & Addons

Drone Flight

We utilize a drone and can provide a multitude of services including

  • Drone footage of a project.
  • Open area topographic survey.
  • High definition orthomosaic image generation.
  • As-built data generation.
Note: This service modifies the timing of other services, inquire with a manager.
Re-Certification

Our certification is only valid for 180 days. After the 180 days, the original requesting party may request a re-certification stating that the land has not gone through another update and we can re-sign the survey.

Survey Update

If a survey is no longer valid due to a minor change in the land, the client has the option to perform a survey update. The client will describe the changes and a PSM will create a scope to limit the field work to changes described.
