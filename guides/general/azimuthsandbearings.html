Introduction

Surveyors use angles in everything we do. Being able to understand and manipulate angles is key in being a successful surveyor. There are three kinds of angles: Azimuths, Deflections, and Bearings. AutoCAD accepts all three formats as valid inputs and has tools to convert between them.
