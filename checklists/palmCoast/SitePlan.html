A. House

Either import the house model from our Homebuilder Houses.dwg file, double checking garage orientation; or draft a house model from the foundation plan of the architectural plans. Rotate the house to match the lot orientation.

Verify the setbacks for the property. Place the building centered between the left and right property lines and behind the front setback line + 1.00 foot.

Provide a building tie on each side of the building (minimum one) perpendicular to that side's property line. The tie will be located on a notable point of the building (building corner or intersection). If a tie falls outside of the property, a building tie is not required.

Extend the driveway lines to meet the edge of pavement line. Keep the flairs of the driveway intact.
