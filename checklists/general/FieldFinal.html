Final Procedures

Final Procedures

Occupy the control that was set in the initial survey. The office will provide the original control points prior to you going to the site. If the original control was destroyed, you can resection and provide the reset control to the office or call Steve.

Locate newly installed and built features on the subject lot, including but not limited to:

  • Driveway and walkway to front door
  • Air Conditioner Pad
  • Electric Meter
  • Cleanouts (and septic tank and drainfield if present)
  • Water meters or new well
  • Culvert or mitered end section

If we have not done a formboard / foundation survey, locate the corners of the house. Provide either a digital or handwritten sketch of the house.

Do a standard topo grid over the subject lot. If the site has new swales on the side or front, include additional shots to adequately show the swale.

Palm Coast The City of Palm Coast requires a 25' grid over the subject property that extends 10' beyond the property line.

If you have any questions, please call Steve or the office.

Office Note: If a lot is on a curve, it is generally harder to tie the boundary to the monuments. Please take extra considerations when working on curved lots.
